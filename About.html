About Me

Beginning Path

My interest in computer programming began when I first started playing video games. I was fascinated by the process of how games were created, which inspired me to start programming in high school. Since then, I have been passionate about coding and its potential.

Current Path

During my time in the Game Programming program at Humber, I worked on many projects that I was truly proud of. However, I wanted to broaden my knowledge of programming, which is why I decided to enroll in the Computer Programming program at Humber. I also realized the importance of maintaining a healthy balance, so I focused on fitness and took up mixed martial arts. It has been a great way to stay in shape and effectively manage the stress that comes with coding.

Future Goals

As I continue to enhance my programming skills, I aspire to work on innovative projects that others can enjoy and benefit from. I also plan to expand my knowledge in various areas of computer science to create meaningful and engaging experiences.
